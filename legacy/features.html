All‑in‑One Roof Health & Quotes

Roofing Marketplace makes roof decisions as simple as checking your credit score: look up your home, analyze your roof with AI, and compare quotes from verified contractors.

For Homeowners

  • Home lookup: Enter your address to automatically pull roof and property details.
  • Satellite roof outline: We detect your roof shape and estimate area from aerial imagery.
  • AI roof health score: 0–100 score with plain‑language explanations.
  • Damage detection: Hail, wind, aging, missing shingles, and more.
  • Repair vs. replace: Clear recommendation based on condition and lifespan.
  • Instant cost ranges: Localized estimates for repair or replacement.
  • Multiple quotes: Compare offers from verified contractors side‑by‑side.

For Contractors

  • Pre‑qualified leads: Homeowners arrive with a roof report and budget expectations.
  • AI takeoffs: Estimated roof area, pitch, and material type.
  • Materials list: Bundles, underlayment, flashing, vents, and labor hours.
  • Quotes workspace: Use AI‑suggested ranges, then finalize your price.
  • Dashboard views: Leads, bids, leaderboard, billing, and insights.

How the Flow Works

  1. Start with Home Lookup to pull property and roof details and satellite outline.
  2. Continue to Upload Roof Photos for AI visual analysis.
  3. We generate a roof health report and cost ranges.
  4. Contractors receive leads with reports and materials lists in their dashboard.

The same AI output powers both the homeowner experience and the contractor tools.
